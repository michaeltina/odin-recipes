Red Velvet Brownies

Description

These chewy red velvet brownies are topped with a tangy cream cheese frosting. Feel free to play around with the amount of cocoa powder in the recipe; use anywhere from 1 to 3 tablespoons, depending on how chocolaty you want them to be.These chewy red velvet brownies are topped with a tangy cream cheese frosting.
